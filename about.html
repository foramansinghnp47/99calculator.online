Our Mission

Our mission is simple: make complex calculations accessible to everyone. Whether you're a student solving a math problem, a homeowner planning a mortgage, a professional estimating ROI, or anyone in between — CalcHub has the right tool for you.

We believe everyone deserves access to accurate, easy-to-use calculators without paywalls, subscriptions, or sign-ups. That's why every single calculator on CalcHub is completely free and always will be.

How We Keep CalcHub Free

CalcHub is funded entirely through advertising. We display non-intrusive ads served by Google AdSense. This allows us to cover our running costs and continue building new calculators — all without charging users a single cent.

We are committed to keeping the ad experience clean and non-disruptive. We will never display pop-ups, auto-play video ads, or deceptive advertising. Our content always comes first.
