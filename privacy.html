Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Summary: CalcHub does not collect personal information. All calculations run entirely in your browser. We do not store your inputs or results. We use Google AdSense and Google Analytics, which may use cookies as described below.

1. Introduction

Welcome to CalcHub ("we", "us", or "our"). We operate the website located at yourcalchub.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and use our free online calculators.

By using CalcHub, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our website.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

CalcHub is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect:

  • Any numbers, figures, or data you enter into our calculators
  • Your calculation results
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifying information
  • Payment or financial information of any kind
  • Account credentials (we have no accounts or sign-ups)

All calculator inputs and outputs are processed entirely within your browser and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Information We Collect Automatically

Like most websites, we collect certain non-personal technical information automatically when you visit:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visit, referring URLs
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage data: Which calculators are used, how long pages are viewed

This information is collected in aggregate and cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us improve our calculators and understand which tools are most useful.

4. Cookies

CalcHub uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics uses cookies to help us understand website traffic and usage patterns. These cookies do not contain personal information.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to show relevant advertisements. These cookies may track your browsing habits across websites to display more relevant ads.
  • Preference cookies: We may use cookies to remember your preferences such as calculator settings.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality. To opt out of Google Analytics, visit tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense is a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC. Google uses cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. For more information on how Google uses data, visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time on site, and browser type. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party website you visit.

8. Children's Privacy

CalcHub is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information on our website, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is minimal security risk to your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all pages. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of CalcHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
